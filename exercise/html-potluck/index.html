Avengers Meme

Avengers Age of Ultron

Movie Rating  |  Show Time: 2hrs, 21min  |  Year Released: 2015

Rise of the Rogue AI

When Tony Stark and Bruce Banner experiment with artificial intelligence to create a global peacekeeping program, they unintentionally give rise to Ultron, a self-aware AI with a warped vision of world peace that involves humanity's extinction. As Ultron amasses an army of robotic minions, the Avengers must reunite to stop his apocalyptic plan. Along the way, they encounter new allies and adversaries, including the superhuman twins Wanda and Pietro Maximoff and the synthetic being Vision. Amidst personal struggles and rising tensions within the team, the Avengers fight to protect the world from destruction, setting the stage for future battles against seven greater threats.

Avengers Infinity War

Movie Rating  |  Show Time: 2hrs, 29min  |  Year Released: 2018

The Mad Titan's Quest

As the Avengers and their allies confront the fallout from their past conflicts, a cosmic threat looms: Thanos, the Mad Titan, embarks on a universe-altering quest to collect all six Infinity Stones. With the power to wipe out half of all life with a snap of his fingers, Thanos seeks to impose his twisted version of balance.

Heroes from across the galaxy—including the Guardians of the Galaxy, Doctor Strange, Spider-Man, and Black Panther—join forces in a desperate fight to stop him. Battles erupt on multiple fronts, from the depths of space to the Wakandan plains, but even their combined strength may not be enough to thwart Thanos' relentless drive. As the Avengers face devastating losses and moral dilemmas, they must grapple with failure and the price of heroism in the face of an unstoppable force.

Avengers End Game

Movie Rating  |  Show Time: 3hrs, 1min  |  Year Released: 2019

The Final Reckoning

After Thanos' devastating snap erased half of all life in the universe, the remaining Avengers are left shattered, grappling with loss and failure. Five years later, a glimmer of hope emerges when Scott Lang (Ant-Man) escapes the quantum realm, presenting the team with a bold and dangerous plan: to travel back in time and collect the Infinity Stones before Thanos can.

Reuniting old allies and facing past challenges, the Avengers embark on a time-heist, revisiting key moments in their history to rewrite the future. As they face emotional reunions and heartbreaking sacrifices, the team unites for one final battle against Thanos, who returns with renewed vengeance.

In a climactic showdown, the Avengers fight not only for their survival but for the very fabric of the universe. With heroism, loss, and triumph at its core, Endgame delivers a powerful conclusion to the Infinity Saga, showcasing the enduring legacy of Earth's mightiest heroes.
